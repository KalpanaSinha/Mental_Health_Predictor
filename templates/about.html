About Us


We are a team of final year B-tech undergraduates from the CSE department of Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology.
We are attempting to build an easy tool for employers to monitor and assist the mental well-being of their employees.

We believe that Our mental health encompasses our psychological, emotional and social well-being. This means it impacts how we feel, think and behave each day. Our mental health also contributes to our decision making process, how we cope with stress and how we relate to others in our lives.
